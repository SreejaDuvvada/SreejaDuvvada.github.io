About Inception

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. It stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious.

Why I Love This Movie

  • Amazing visual effects and cinematography
  • Complex and thought-provoking storyline
  • Outstanding performances by the cast
